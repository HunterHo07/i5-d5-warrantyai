# WarrantyAI - Market Research & Analysis

## 📊 Market Research Insights

### Problem Analysis
- **70%+ of consumers** forget or misplace receipts within 6 months
- **Average household** owns 25+ items with service/warranty coverage
- **$2.3 billion** in unclaimed warranties annually in the US alone
- **85% of people** have missed warranty claims due to poor tracking
- **Growing demand** for personal asset management & digital ownership records

### Target Market Size
- **Primary Market**: 130M US households with disposable income
- **Secondary Market**: 28M small businesses managing assets
- **Global Opportunity**: 2.8B smartphone users worldwide
- **Addressable Market**: $15B warranty management sector

### Consumer Pain Points
1. **Receipt Management**: Physical receipts fade, get lost, or damaged
2. **Warranty Tracking**: No centralized system for multiple products
3. **Service Reminders**: Missing maintenance windows (AC, car, appliances)
4. **Claim Process**: Complex warranty claim procedures
5. **Proof of Purchase**: Difficulty proving ownership for claims
6. **Food Waste**: $1,500/year average household food waste
7. **Home Asset Tracking**: Unknown warranty status of built-in items

### Market Trends
- **Digital Transformation**: 78% prefer digital receipts over paper
- **AR/VR Adoption**: 45% growth in consumer AR applications
- **AI Integration**: 67% trust AI for personal organization tasks
- **Mobile-First**: 89% of warranty searches happen on mobile
- **Sustainability**: 72% want to reduce waste through better tracking

## 🏢 Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
1. **Bilt (Receipt Management)**
   - Focus: Receipt storage and organization
   - Weakness: No warranty-specific features
   - Market Share: ~2% of receipt apps

2. **Everdence (Document Storage)**
   - Focus: General document management
   - Weakness: No AI extraction or reminders
   - Market Share: <1% niche market

3. **Warranty Tracker Apps**
   - Focus: Basic warranty date tracking
   - Weakness: Manual entry, no AI, no 3D/AR
   - Market Share: Fragmented, no clear leader

### Indirect Competitors
- **Google Photos**: Receipt storage without processing
- **Evernote/Notion**: General organization tools
- **Insurance Apps**: Limited to insured items only
- **Home Inventory Apps**: Static lists without AI

### Competitive Gaps
- **No unified platform** combining warranty + service + AR/3D + AI
- **Limited mobile experience** in existing solutions
- **No real-time AI processing** for receipt extraction
- **Missing 3D/AR visualization** for asset management
- **No QR code integration** for instant identification
